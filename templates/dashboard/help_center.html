Help center

1. Quick navigation
  • Use breadcrumbs on each page to know where you are.
  • Use the quick bar buttons: Go back, Go to dashboard, and Help center.
  • Fleet features are under Fleet, maintenance and stock under Maintenance.
2. Preventing mistakes
  • Dangerous actions ask for confirmation before continuing.
  • Forms validate fields and highlight invalid values.
  • If you edit a form and try to leave, the app warns about unsaved changes.
3. Error recovery
  • System messages (top-right) explain successful or failed actions.
  • On permission errors, check your role or try a route available to your profile.
  • If telemetry pages look empty, verify live telemetry is running and date filters are correct.
Recommended user flows
Fleet manager flow
  1. Open Fleet → Vehicles and select a vehicle.
  2. Review Sensors, GPS, Driving and Mileage panels.
  3. Open Maintenance → Tasks to schedule work.
  4. Use Maintenance → Spare parts to adjust stock if needed.
Reports and decisions flow
  1. Open Reports & predictions.
  2. Check failure predictions, review criticality reasons, and override only with a clear reason.
  3. Review suggested maintenance, adjust the proposed date/priority, then create the task.
  4. Download PDF reports or use Export Center for spreadsheet-ready data.
  5. Administrators should review Settings → Audit log after critical operations.
